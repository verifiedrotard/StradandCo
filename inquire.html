Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the import process take?

Typically 4-8 weeks from purchase to delivery, depending on origin country, shipping method, and customs clearance. We provide timeline estimates for your specific situation.

Do you handle all customs paperwork?

Yes, we manage all documentation including customs forms, compliance certificates, EPA/DOT requirements, and any other necessary paperwork for your jurisdiction.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary based on vehicle value, origin, and services required. We provide transparent quotes with no hidden costs. Contact us for a detailed breakdown.

Can you source specific vehicles?

Absolutely! We have access to dealers, auctions, and private sellers worldwide. Tell us your requirements and budget, and we'll find the perfect match.

Do you offer inspection services?

Yes, we arrange pre-purchase inspections through trusted local partners to ensure the vehicle condition meets your expectations before purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, letters of credit, and can arrange escrow services for your security. Payment terms are flexible based on transaction size.

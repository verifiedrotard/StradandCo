About STRAD & Co.

Your trusted partner in global automotive trade since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts with a passion for connecting buyers and sellers across borders, STRAD and Co. has grown from a small import business to a global leader in automotive trade.

What started as a mission to help individuals import their dream vehicles has evolved into a comprehensive service offering that spans import, export, sourcing, and complete logistics management. Our success is built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we work with private buyers, collectors, dealers, and commercial fleet operators in over 40 countries, facilitating thousands of vehicle transactions each year. Despite our growth, we've maintained the personal touch and attention to detail that defined us from the beginning.
